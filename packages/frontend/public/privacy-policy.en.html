Politique de confidentialité - Version française

1. Data Collection

  • We collect data only to provide you with features
  • No data collected if you do not create an account, your history is then saved on your device
  • If you create an account: we collect your login and meditation history.
  • No sharing of your data with third parties guaranteed since we do not collect personal identifiers like email

Creating an account allows you to save your meditation history on our server, so you can access it from multiple devices and recover it if you lose your device, if you uninstall or clear the app data, and if you clear your web browser data

2. Security

Your data is stored securely (HTTPS protocols, secure databases, hosting provider: https://www.koyeb.com).

3. Your Rights

According to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you can at any time:

  • Delete your account (and all your data) by requesting it
  • Request a copy of your data

4. Cookies

  • No cookies used
  • IndexDb is used instead of cookies to save data on your device and enable offline use of the application

5. Changes

This policy may be updated. You will be notified in the application.
